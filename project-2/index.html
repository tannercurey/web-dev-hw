How to Make Caprese

Recipe by Tanner Curey

My bones are made of parmigiano reggiano, and balsamic vinegar rushes through my veins.

- Chef Massimo Bottura

Preparation

  1. Only buy the freshest ingredients.
    • Play the song Tomato Mozzarella Salad by Italian Social Club for inspiration.
  2. Wash your hands!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh Uncut Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Baguette
    • Avocado can be substituted for baguette to make recipe gluten free.
⇒ Proceed to Instructions
