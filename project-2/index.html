How to Make Caprese

Recipe by Tanner Curey

My bones are made of parmigiano reggiano, and balsamic vinegar rushes through my veins.

- Chef Massimo Bottura

Preparation

Play the song Tomato Mozzarella Salad by Italian Social Club and wash your hands!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh Uncut Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Baguette
    • Avocado can be substituted for baguette to make recipe gluten free.

    Directions

    1. Cut tomato into flat round slices.
    2. Cut Mozzarella into flat round slices.
    3. Slice baguette into individual slices.
    4. Place individual mozzarella slice on top of a slice of baguette, place tomato slice on top of mozzarella, and garnish with basil leaf on top.
    5. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the caprese.
    6. Enjoy!
      • May be enjoyed uncooked or toasted. To toast, preheat oven to 450 and bake for 5 minutes.
