Instructions

Step by Step Directions
Step 1 Cut tomato into flat round slices.
Step 2 Cut mozzarella into flat round slices.
Step 3 Slice baguette into individual slices.
Step 4 Place individual mozzarella slice on top of a slice of baguette, place tomato slice on top of mozzarella, and garnish with basil leaf on top.
Step 5 Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the caprese.
Step 6 Enjoy! May be enjoyed uncooked or toasted. To toast, preheat oven to 450° and bake for 5 min.
